ABOUT US

What this website is about.

We’re trying to share some information about the hydrometeorological hazards with our skills and creativity. Most of our content is a long-form of hydrometeorological hazards, its impending signs, preparedness and mitigation, and the tools used for monitoring hydrometeorological hazards. Our purpose here is to help people be aware of this kinds of hazards and how to deal with them before, during and after it occurs.
